NoChe

Visit historic NoChe

The neighborhood north of Chelsea, better known as NoChe is abuts Hell's Kitchen to the North and Midtown Manhatttan to the East. It is a must visit location, bustling with activity at all times, and those who visit are never disappointed.

In NoChe, you can get on the scenic High Line park or walk down the Hudson River Greenway. When hungry, check out New York's amazing street food, or if you clamor for a more refined experience, dine at one of NoChe's world famous restaurants.
